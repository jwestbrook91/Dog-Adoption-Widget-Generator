Iso may be mature, but she still has lots of pep in her step! She loves to romp around and get lots of exercise, but then snuggle up with you for some cuddle time. She needs daily mental and physical exercise to keep her mind and body in check. She is looking for a quiet home in a low dog traffic neighborhood. If you have another dog bring them along to meet Iso! Come meet this goofy girl today!
